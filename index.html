About me

I am an italian Ph.D. student in astronomy at the Institute de Recherche en Astrophysique et Planétologie (IRAP) in Toulouse, France. As of January 2022, I am also a one-year visiting researcher at European Space Research and Technology Centre (ESTEC) in Noordwijk, Netherlands, under an international co-supervision project.

I previously obtained a Physics B.Sc degree in February 2017 at the University of Pavia, Italy and then a Physics M.Sc degree with specialisation in Astrophysics in August 2019 at the Niels Böhr Institute, Copenhagen, Denmark.

My research is co-funded by the Agence Nationale de Recherche (ANR) within the SPLaSH (SPIRou Planet Search for Habitable worlds) programme and the European Space Agency (ESA).
